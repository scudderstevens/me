Process & Results

Problems to Solve

Jobs to Be Done, statements:
  • Accuracy
    • Accuracy

    Respondents cited challenges verifying research data as correct, the complexities of determining accurate values, and time wasted when values or information were determined to be inaccurate.

  • Frequent Change
    • Change

    The biggest challenges tax researchers face is keeping up with the ever-changing tax landscape. "As the laws change frequently, it takes time to learn these changes, doing it quickly and accurately …"

  • Lack of Transparency
    • Transparency

    Respondents voiced confusion and irritation around finding, and understanding, the answers to their tax queries in plain and simple language. "…it (currently used services) has no direct answers; you have to continuously depend on other sources …"

  • Different Needs
    • Needs

    Tax experts faced challenges managing client's communication needs and tax goals regarding the products they offer. Users need a way of managing their data and research in a way that optimizes their time and further builds their relationship with key stakeholders.

How Might We, statements:
    • Accuracy

    How might we help users know the answers they receive are as accurate as possible?

    • Change

    How might we help users stay up to date on tax changes?

    • Transparency

    How might we help users obtain straightforward answers to their tax inquiries?

    • Needs

    How might we help users prioritize what client/business needs to address?

Personas

  • Tax Attorney

    Help their clients stay compliant at the local, state and federal levels and keep them current on tax certifications and laws.

  • Tax Consultant

    Help their clients stay compliant at the local, state and federal levels and keep them current on tax certifications and laws.

  • Tax Accountant

    Support their company's compliance obligations at the local, state and federal levels. Collects and accurately pays indirect taxes and stays current on tax certifications and laws.

  • CFO

    Ensure their company stays compliant at the local, state and federal levels. Remains current on tax certifications and laws to make better business decisions using tax information.
